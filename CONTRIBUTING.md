# Contributing

This repository is a **read-only transparency mirror** of Entwine's internal
development of the Lucy Android app. It is published so the public can audit what the
app does — not as a collaborative project.

- **Pull requests and issues are not monitored** and will not be merged or answered.
- The source of truth lives in Entwine's private monorepo; this mirror is regenerated
  automatically at each release, so external commits here would be overwritten.

## Security disclosures

If you believe you have found a security or privacy issue, please **do not open a public
issue**. Contact Entwine Health through the details at <https://entwine.health>. We take
disclosures about a patient-facing health app seriously and will respond.
